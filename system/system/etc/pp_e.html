I.       How we Collect and Use Personal Information

"Personal information" refers to any information related to an identified or identifiable natural person. An Identifiable natural person refers to the natural person who can be directly or indirectly identified its identification by name, ID number, location data, network identification symbols, economic, cultural, social identity and other identifiers.

Personal information that we collect is mainly divided into the following types:

1)      Account information

When you plan to register for a Nubia-related service account, you need to provide a mobile phone number or email for identity verification, and you can also provide supplementary information, such as avatar, nickname, birthday, etc. When you use a third-party (such as Facebook, Twitter, WeChat) account to log in to Nubia’s business platform (You can also choose to refuse to access our products or services in this way), with user’s authorization, we will obtain your verification information from the third-party server. Our server will also keep the access tokens granted by third-party platforms and share them among the specific services used by users to prevent users from repeatedly logging in to third-party servers.

2)      Device information

When some services are for specific device models (such as certain models of mobile phones) or applications, we will collect device-related information before and during service activation, including the user's device model, system version, unique device identification code, network status, application activity information, abnormal information, and IP address information, so as to accurately match the required services.

3)      Location information

When you use Nubia terminal business software with positioning function (such as family positioning, mobile phone retrieval), based on your consent, we may collect and process information about the actual location of the user/terminal device, but we will not continuously collect your location information to track your whereabouts.

4)      Order information

If you need to shop on our website, the system will need to collect information about your delivery address, consignee, contact number, zip code, and the goods or services you purchased when generating an order.

5)      After-sales information

When you need us to provide after-sales service or when you feedback the problems encountered in use, we may collect your name, phone number, equipment information, fault description and other necessary information required for after-sales service. You can also provide us with information according to the service items you need. For example, when you need an after-sales mailing service, you need provide the mailing address and consignee information.

6)      Access services information

We will also use the information collection function of the product to record some behavioral information when you visit ZTE-related services, including access time, search query terms, network identification information, IP address, etc., and in order to provide precise and personalized service, we will also obtain data about you from legal public and commercial sources when permitted by law.

7) Other information

Some service items, such as cloud backup and cloud storage provided by Nubia APP, may save the user's address book, text messages, photos and other information on the server. If the user does not want to provide such information, he/she can refuse, but may not be able to use the related service or can only partially use the related service function. When you apply for a job on our website, we may ask you to provide your job resume.

Sensitive personal information is a special type of personal information, usually defined as various types of information related to the following aspects: race or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, philosophical beliefs, union membership, personal genetic data, biometric data, health data, sexual life, sexual orientation, etc. In some cases, personal sensitive information may also include criminal charges or sentences, precise geographic location information, financial and bank account numbers, and unique identifiers such as government-issued social security numbers, driving licenses, and passport numbers.

When we need to collect or process sensitive personal information, for example, if you plan to purchase products from us or order our services, we may collect payment-related sensitive personal information such as bank card numbers. In this case, we will process them in accordance with applicable legal requirements, such as seeking your consent before collection.

VII. How we Use Cookies and Related Technologies

Our website uses cookies and other similar technologies to distinguish you from other users of our website. This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse the website and allows us to improve our website.

Cookies are usually categorized as "session" cookies or "persistent" cookies. Session cookies can help you navigate the website effectively, tracking your page-to-page progress, so that you are not required to repeatedly provide information during your current visit. Session cookies are stored in temporary memory and are deleted when the web browser is closed. Persistent cookies store user preferences and are stored on your device and remain valid when the browser is restarted. For example, we use persistent cookies to record the language and country you choose, font size, or other settings. This saves you from having to reconfigure the next time you visit our website.

You can clear all cookies stored on your computer or delete cookies stored on your device directly, but if you do so, you will need to change your settings every time you visit our website. Most browsers have the function of blocking cookies. If you want to manage cookies through your browser, you can refer to the browser developer's introduction or search from the Internet. For more information on cookies, you can also check out (Nubia Cookies Policy).
