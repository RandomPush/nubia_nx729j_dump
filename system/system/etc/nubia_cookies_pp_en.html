Cookies Policy

Information about the cookies we use

Our website uses cookies and other similar technologies to distinguish you from other users on our website. This helps us to provide you with a good experience when you browse the website and allows us to improve our website.

Cookie is a small file of letters and numbers. When you agree, we store it in the memory or hard drive of your computer or mobile device, and then use it as a label to identify the computer or mobile device.

Cookies are generally classified as "session" cookies or "persistent" cookies. Session cookies can help you browse the website effectively and track your page-to-page progress, so that you are not required to provide information repeatedly during your current visit, for example, your shopping cart will not be emptied when you switch pages. Session cookies are stored in temporary memory and are deleted when the web browser is closed. Persistent cookies store the user's preferences, which are stored on your device and are still valid when the browser is restarted. For example, we use persistent cookies to record your selected language and country/region, font size or other settings. This can save you from reconfiguration the next time you visit our website.

You can manage cookies through your browser, or directly delete cookies stored on your device, and most browsers have the function of blocking cookies. We do not use cookies for other purposes as described above.

Nubia will not use cookies for any purpose other than those described in this policy. You can manage or delete cookies according to your preferences. For more information about cookies, you can check the AboutCookies.org website. You can clear all cookies saved on your computer, and most web browsers have a feature to block cookies. However, if you do this, you will need to change the settings every time you visit our website. To learn more about how to change the browser settings, please contact the browser developer you are using or check on the Internet.

In addition to cookies, we also use other similar technologies such as web beacons and pixel tags on our website. For example, the account activation link we sent to your email. The above technologies allow us to track this click to help us understand your product and service preferences and improve customer service. A web beacon is usually a transparent image embedded in a website or email. With the help of the pixel tags in the email, we can know whether the email has been opened. If you do not want your activities to be tracked in this way, you can set your email address to refuse acceptance at any time, or log on to our relevant service website to unsubscribe from this mail service.

If your browser has the "Do Not Track" feature, our service website will respect your choice.

We use the following cookies:

Strictly required Cookies: These cookies are required for the operation of our website. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of our website, use shopping carts, or use electronic billing services.

Analytics/Performance Cookies: It allows us to identify and calculate the number of visitors and to understand how visitors move when using the site. This helps us improve the way the website works. For example, ensure that users can easily find what they're looking for.

Functional Cookies: These cookies are used to identify you when you return to our website. This allows us to provide you with personalized content, welcome you by name, and remember your preferences (for example, the language or region you choose).

Targeted Cookies: These cookies record the information about your visit to our website, the pages you visit and the links you enter. We will use this information to make our website and the ads displayed on it more in line with your interests. For this reason, we may also share this information with third parties.
